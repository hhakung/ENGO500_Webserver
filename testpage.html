Welcome to our project...

All fourth year engineering students at the University of Calgary take part in a Capstone Design project. For our project, we are presenting information about the study space environments on the U of C main campus. To complete this project, we have created sensor units to collect data about the Wi-Fi speed, temperature, and noise levels of the area surrounding the unit. We have placed a number of these units around campus in common study areas. This data is then presented in a map for easy viewing, allowing users to remotely view the status of each study space and decide which space is suitable for their preferences.
